Services

The right therapy

At Hersee Massage, we offer targeted massage treatments that relieve stress and physical tension. Our skilled therapists identify the root cause of your discomfort and provide comprehensive care to help you achieve optimal health and wellness.

We believe in addressing the body as a whole, balancing not only your muscles and bones, but also your energetic, emotional, and nutritional systems. From headaches to injuries, our treatments provide lasting relief for a variety of conditions.

Choose Hersee Massage for relief from muscle tension, chronic pain, digestive issues, asthma, fatigue, and more. Let us help you achieve complete wellness and fully enjoy life.

Treatments Offered

Foot Massage

Benefits

Foot massage relaxes the feet and reduces stress, Improves blood circulation.

Helps alleviate plantar fasciitis and reduces pain and stiffness in the feet and lower legs.

Sports Massage

Benefits

Sports massage improves athletic performance, reduces pain, prevents injury, speeds up recovery, and promotes relaxation in just one session.

Deep Tissue Massage

Benefits

Deep tissue massage targets deep layers of muscles and connective tissues to alleviate chronic muscle tension and pain.

It can also improve range of motion, reduce inflammation and stress, and promote relaxation.

Pregnancy Massage

Benefits

Pregnancy massage can reduce anxiety, relieve muscle and joint pain, improve sleep, and decrease the risk of premature birth.

It's a safe and effective way to support the physical and emotional well-being of expectant mothers.

Relaxation Massage

Benefits

Relaxation massage provides a soothing and calming experience, promoting deep relaxation and reducing stress and anxiety.

It can improve circulation, relieve muscle tension, and enhance overall well-being.

Trigger Point Therapy

Benefits

Trigger point massage targets tight areas in muscles that can cause pain and discomfort.

It helps release tension and alleviate pain in specific areas,improve flexibility, increase range of motion, and improve overall performance.

Particularly effective for those suffering from chronic pain or injuries

Head, Neck & Shoulder

Benefits

A head, neck, and shoulder massage can relieve tension, reduce headaches, improve circulation, and increase relaxation.

Also alleviate stiffness and promote better posture, and with regular sessions, clients can experience long-term benefits for their overall physical and mental well-being.

Lymphatic Drainage

Benefits

Lymphatic drainage massage can boost the immune system, reduce swelling and inflammation, and improve circulation.

It can also aid in detoxification and alleviate symptoms of conditions such as lymphedema, fibromyalgia, and chronic fatigue syndrome.
