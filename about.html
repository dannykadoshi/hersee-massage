About me

Tara Hersee

I'm Tara Hersee, a highly qualified and experienced massage therapist with over 10 years of hands-on experience in the field. Providing my clients with the highest level of service is my top priority, and I take great pride in doing so with a professional yet warm approach.

Having pursued various trainings and qualifications, I bring a deep understanding of anatomy and physiology to my massage practice. My expertise covers a range of massage techniques, including Swedish massage, deep tissue massage, sports massage, pregnancy massage, and lymphatic drainage.

I'm proud to say that my clients have always been happy with the positive outcomes of my treatments. Whether you're looking for relief from a particular issue or simply want to relax and de-stress, I'm confident that I have the knowledge, skill, and intuition to help you achieve your goals. So why not book a session with me today and experience the transformative power of massage firsthand?
